CONTEXT
The Standard

How We Handle
The Text

Every episode follows the same discipline. Who wrote it. Why they wrote it. What the words actually meant in their original context. Then and only then, what it means for us today. The text always goes first.

Step 01
Historical Context
Who wrote it, who received it, and what was happening in the world when they did.
Step 02
Linguistic Precision
What the original words actually meant. Not what we want them to mean.
Step 03
Honest Application
What the passage asks of us when we've understood it correctly.
