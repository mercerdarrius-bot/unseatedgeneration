The Platform

About
Unseated Generation.

This is not a platform built around a personality. It is a platform built around a burden. A burden for the people of God to be free, formed, and walking in the fullness of their identity in Christ.

BURDEN
The Burden

The Church Has Drifted.

Many of us were never taught any differently. What we call church culture is simply the water we grew up in. Performance wasn't chosen. It was inherited. And what is inherited feels like truth until something or Someone shows you otherwise.

The desire to be financially prosperous, to have huge followings, to carry enormous influence — these things were presented to us as the fruit of faithfulness. But all of it is meaningless apart from the transformative power of Christ.

This is the burden Unseated Generation was built to address. Not to condemn the people formed by these systems, but to offer what the Holy Spirit offered us — a clearer picture of what God actually intended.

The Problem
Most people in performative culture are sincere. They love God genuinely. But sincerity without formation produces people who are devoted and yet still distant from the depth God intended.
The Pattern
When performance is all you have seen modeled, it becomes your standard. Not by rebellion but by formation. The environment shapes us more than we realize.
The Drift
This is not about bad intentions. It is about inherited frameworks. We carry what we were given until the Holy Spirit shows us something better.
CALL
The Assignment

To Awaken the Remnant.

The assignment of Unseated Generation is to call the church back into alignment with its identity. To expose what has occupied the seat that belongs to God and to call for the rebuilding of the sacred altar.

That calling moves through a specific confrontation, of the systems, patterns, and mindsets that prevent alignment with God. Because the remnant cannot walk in their assignment until what has formed them out of alignment has been named, confronted, and surrendered. This is preparation. Confrontation is not the end. It is the doorway to the call God has placed on this generation and to being part of what He is doing in this hour.

That rebuilding does not happen through better programming or larger platforms. It happens through surrender to God, offering our bodies as living sacrifices, and returning to the truth of Scripture handled with integrity and reverence.

01
Surrender to God
Not to a system. Not to a movement. Not to a personality. Full surrender to the Lordship of Christ over every area of life and ministry.
02
Rebuild the Altar
The altar of genuine relationship with God. Of daily surrender. Of private devotion that has no audience. Of letting formation do its work.
03
Return to Truth
Scripture handled with rigor, reverence, and honesty. Not mined for comfort but received for formation. The text prosecutes first.

The Founder
Darrius
Founder · Unseated Generation

Like many, Darrius walked in the ignorance that comes from performative church culture. He made his faith self-centered, took scripture out of context, and built a version of Christianity shaped more by the environment around him than by the Word of God.

In his own personal study, led by the Holy Spirit, he began to see how far the church had drifted and how he himself had been formed by things that contradicted Scripture. That discovery became a burden. That burden became an assignment.

Unseated Generation launched in 2022. Life happened. Discouragement came. But the burden never left. Now more grounded, more equipped, and more certain of the call than ever before, he moves forward not in his own ability but in the identity and anointing he carries in Christ.
